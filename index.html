About Me

Hello! I'm Liam Hodgins, a passionate GIS professional dedicated to leveraging the power of open-source mapping tools to solve real-world geographical challenges. With a strong foundation in spatial analysis, data visualization, and web mapping, I enjoy transforming complex datasets into insightful and actionable information.

My expertise includes working with tools like QGIS for desktop analysis, Leaflet.js and OpenLayers for interactive web maps, and PostGIS for robust spatial data management. I'm always eager to explore new technologies and methodologies to enhance my GIS capabilities.

This portfolio showcases some of my recent projects where I've applied these skills to deliver impactful results.

Developed a suitability model for identifying optimal locations for solar panel installations using various environmental and socio-economic factors. Visualized results using OpenLayers.

Public Transit Accessibility Study

Modeled and mapped forest fire risk zones based on vegetation type, topography, climate data, and proximity to human settlements. The interactive map allows for dynamic querying of risk levels.
